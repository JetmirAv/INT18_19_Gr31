Flights

Economy

Whether you call it coach, standard, main cabin, or "cattle class", economy is the most basic class.The seats here are the narrowest, ranging from 16 inches to just over 19 inches wide and the seat pitch,the distance from one seat to the seat in front or behind it-what travelers often refer to as "legroom"-ranges from 30 inches to 34 inches

  • Baggage dimensions 56x45x25
  • No drink included
  • No meat included

Premium economy

Premium economy refers to the "better" seats on an aircraft on domestic flights, it also offer additional perks like better food,a greater baggage allowance and amenity kit.

  • Baggage max. weight 15kg
  • Drink included(Tea/Coffee)
  • Full meal service

Passengers can't get upgraded any further than first class, which is a luxurious experience that varies depending on the airline. Business

Business Hotels are the largest group of hotel types and they primarily cater to business travellers and usually located in downtown or business districts. Guest amenities at business hotels may include complimentary newspapers, morning coffee, free local telephone calls, Breakfast etc.
